About FoodSaver

Building a Sustainable Future

Born from the collaboration between Haldia Institute of Technology and the Government of West Bengal, FoodSaver represents a commitment to leveraging technology for social good and environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable food ecosystem where no edible food goes to waste while ensuring those in need have access to nutritious meals.

Our Vision

A world where technology bridges the gap between food surplus and food insecurity, creating communities without hunger.

Our Innovation

Leveraging AI and real-time matching to create the most efficient food redistribution network in the world.
